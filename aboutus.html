★ Our Story ★

Candy hunters.
Worldwide.

We didn't invent candy. We just got really, really good at finding the best of it.

★ The Why ★

Best candy is
somewhere else.

Have you ever had a Hi-Chew straight from a Tokyo combini? Or Mexican Pelon Pelo Rico from a corner store in Mexico City? It hits different. Way different.

Most candy in Dutch supermarkets is the same five brands you've had since you were 6. There's a whole world out there — Japanese mochi gummies, Korean watermelon bars, Brazilian peanut candy, American sour rope.

We bring it all to you.

★ The Build ★

How we got here.

2024

The idea

A friend brought back a suitcase from Tokyo full of candy. We tasted everything in one sitting. We knew Dutch shelves were missing this.

2025

The brand

RYDE was born. Logo, website, vibe — all dialed in. Started talking to suppliers in Japan, Mexico, USA, Korea.

2026

The launch

First mystery boxes shipped. Slicks and Drifters series in development. The ride is just getting started.
